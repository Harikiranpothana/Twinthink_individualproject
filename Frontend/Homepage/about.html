About TwinThink

TwinThink is an AI-powered digital twin designed to preserve, organize, and enhance personal knowledge by transforming scattered information into meaningful insights.

Our Vision

To create an intelligent personal memory system that empowers individuals to learn continuously, rediscover forgotten knowledge, and make better decisions through AI.

Our Mission

TwinThink aims to bridge the gap between human memory and artificial intelligence by building a personalized knowledge companion that evolves with its users.

Why TwinThink?

People often forget valuable information scattered across notes, documents, and learning resources. TwinThink helps users retain, retrieve, and understand their knowledge effectively.
